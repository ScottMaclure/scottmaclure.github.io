About

I've loved working with the web since the earliest days of my profession. The open, rapid nature of development means the cost of experimenting is low. Risks can be taken. Agile excels here (scrum, kanban).

In recent years, I've appreciated the rise in focus on quality in the web stack, and recent advances with multi-device development (including native), which allows for rich, consistent and fast user experiences.

In my current role as Applications Architect, I'm always excited by the continuous challenges and learning this industry demands, and working with my colleagues to implement customer-focused, pragmatic solutions. There's nothing more exciting than making people's lives easier through technology.
